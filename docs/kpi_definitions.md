# KPI Definitions

_Generated by `python -m mill docs`. Executable definitions live in `src/mill/warehouse/kpi.py`; BI-facing copies in `sql/views`._

| KPI | Grain | Formula | Source tags | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Throughput | shift / day / hour | tonnes_milled = Σ(feed_throughput_tph × interval_h) | feed_throughput_tph | Metallurgy |
| Mill availability % | shift | 100 × running_minutes / scheduled_minutes | feed_throughput_tph (running when > 0) | Operations |
| Mill utilization % | shift | 100 × avg_running_throughput / nameplate_tph (OEE performance factor) | feed_throughput_tph | Operations |
| Recovery % | shift | tonnage-weighted mean of recovery_pct over valid minutes | recovery_pct, feed_throughput_tph | Metallurgy |
| Head grade (g/t) | shift | tonnage-weighted mean of feed_grade_gpt | feed_grade_gpt, feed_throughput_tph | Geology |
| Tails grade (g/t) | shift | tonnage-weighted mean of tails_grade_gpt | tails_grade_gpt, feed_throughput_tph | Metallurgy |
| Specific energy (kWh/t) | shift | Σ(mill_power_kw × interval_h) / tonnes_milled | mill_power_kw, feed_throughput_tph | Operations |
| Cyanide consumption (kg/t) | shift | tonnage-weighted mean of cyanide_dose_kgpt | cyanide_dose_kgpt, feed_throughput_tph | Metallurgy |
| Lime consumption (kg/t) | shift | tonnage-weighted mean of lime_dose_kgpt | lime_dose_kgpt, feed_throughput_tph | Metallurgy |
| Gold poured (oz) | shift / cumulative | Σ gold_poured_oz (per shift), cumulative running total | gold_poured_oz | Gold Room |
| Gold in feed / recovered / tails (oz) | shift | Σ(tonnes × grade)/31.1035 for each stream; recovered = feed × recovery; reconciliation: feed ≈ recovered + tails | feed_throughput_tph, feed_grade_gpt, recovery_pct, tails_grade_gpt | Metallurgy |
| OEE-style composite % | shift | availability × utilization × quality / 10000, quality = % Good historian flags | all of the above + quality_flag | Operations |
